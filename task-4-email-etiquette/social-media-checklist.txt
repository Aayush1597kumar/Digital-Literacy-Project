SOCIAL MEDIA DO's AND DON'Ts

DO's:
1. Use social media in a positive and respectful way
2. Think before posting anything online
3. Keep your profile clean and professional
4. Use privacy settings to protect your personal information
5. Share useful and meaningful content
6. Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
7. Verify information before sharing it
8. Use social media for learning and gaining knowledge
9. Keep your account secure with strong passwords
10. Report any fake or suspicious activity

DON'Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
3. Do not engage in arguments or cyberbullying
4. Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people
5. Do not spread fake news or misinformation
6. Do not use abusive or disrespectful language
7. Avoid wasting too much time on social media
8. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
9. Do not post anything that can harm your reputation
10. Do not misuse others’ content without permission
