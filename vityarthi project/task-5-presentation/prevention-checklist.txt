STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
2. Always check carefully before accepting any payment request
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
4. Use only official and trusted payment applications
5. Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
6. Regularly check your bank transactions
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
8. Be careful of calls claiming to be from bank officials
9.Do not save your bank details on unknown websites
10. Keep your mobile phone and apps updated regularly
11. Log out from apps after using them on shared devices
12. Do not respond to unknown messages asking for money

13. Report any fraud on cybercrime.gov.in
14. Call helpline number 1930 immediately in case of fraud.
